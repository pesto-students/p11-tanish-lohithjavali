Edo - ITC Gardenia

⭐⭐⭐⭐⭐

Overview

Cuisine: Japanese

Must haves: Beer, Sashimi, Set Menu, Salmon

Price: ~ ₹3000

Located within the ITC Gardenia Hotel, Edo Restaurant is an authentic Japanese eatery serving a wide variety of sumptuous dishes in Bangalore. Try their shabu-shabu (a Japanese-style hot pot) and the sukiyaki. This best restaurant in Bangalore is minimalist in design and oozes comfort with its relaxing décor. Each dish here is outstanding, in taste and preparation, making it a great choice for a special occasion celebration.
